Our Story


We met in June, 2013, at a party neither of us was going to go to. Kasia knew it would be full of IT geeks, and, being an arts graduate, she thought it might a bit boring. Kristof wasn’t going to go either because... well, it was going to be full of guys doing IT!


But we both came - and it was love at first sight.



We dated throughout the summer - our first trip together was to the Peak District. We’ve also travelled to Mallorca, Morocco, Norway, Greece and did a road trip across the Balkans with friends.



We moved in together in January 2015. In Surbiton we enjoy having beautiful parks, the Thames riverwalk and traditional villages nearby - and of course having visitors!



Kristof proposed by the river Thames on our way home after a dinner in a restaurant in December 2015 - Kasia of course said yes!
